About Me

We are the largest organization in Helsinki

We is a dedicated group of marine conservationists committed to preserving the local body of waters and the diverse life they sustain. Founded in 2023, we are based in Helsinki, Finland Our mission is to protect and restore marine ecosystems, promoting the sustainability of our oceans for future generations. We believe in the power of research, education, policy change, and community engagement to drive our conservation efforts.

Our team comprises marine biologists, environmental scientists, policy advocates, educators, and volunteers who share a common passion for the sea. We work tirelessly to combat the challenges facing our oceans, including pollution, climate change, overfishing, and habitat destruction.
